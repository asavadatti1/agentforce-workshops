About These Workshops

Welcome to the central hub for Agentforce Workshop TAs. This repository is built by TAs, for TAs, to streamline how we manage, track, and scale Agentforce workshops across the board. Whether you are prepping for your first session or you're a seasoned TA looking to leverage proven resources, this is our collective launchpad for enabling developers and admins on Salesforce's autonomous AI platform.

Why This Repository Exists:

  • Track Every Session: A uniied ledger to log upcoming and past workshops, attendee metrics, and common friction points.
  • Leverage Collective Knowledge: Don't reinvent the wheel. Access battle-tested lab setups, custom troubleshooting scripts, and slide decks used by fellow TAs.
  • Continuous Improvement: Submit pull requests to update lab instructions based on real-time platform updates or student feedback.

TA Note: Before running your next session, check the #/workshops directory to see what variations or custom org configurations other TAs have successfully deployed..

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced TA, there's a workshop for you.
